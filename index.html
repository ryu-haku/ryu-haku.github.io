About

Ryuhaku lab explores new ways to evaluate company value by combining traditional financial analysis with ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) perspectives.

We are particularly interested in how companies are changing to achieve zero carbon emissions. For example, in the steel industry, companies face environmental challenges like reducing carbon emissions in their production processes. By carefully analyzing both their environmental investments and financial impacts, we can find paths toward sustainable growth.

We share our research findings not only with academics but also with business professionals through newspaper articles and lectures. We hope to make a small contribution to sustainable social and economic development.
