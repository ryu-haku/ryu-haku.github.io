About

Our research focuses on environmental management, accounting and finance, with an emphasis on sustainable economy.

We analyze the relationship between corporate environmental protection strategies and profitability, the impact of corporate environmental information disclosure on investor and consumer decision-making, and the feasibility of investing in environmentally sensitive projects to alleviate environmental problems. The goal of our research is to promote the sustainable practice of enterprises and financial investment institutions in order to realize a society with environmental symbiosis.

Sustainability is our top priority. We will continue to work towards this goal and have a positive impact on the world.
